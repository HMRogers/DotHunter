## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Hunt every dot. Miss nothing.

DotHunter is a neon-lit reaction game that tests your speed, focus, and color perception across three intense game modes. Glowing dots appear on screen — tap them before time runs out. Sounds simple? It gets harder every round.

THREE GAME MODES

Classic Focus
One dot. Pure speed. Tap the glowing dot as fast as you can. Each round adds more dots and less time. How far can you go?

Color Filter
Green dots and decoy dots flood the screen. Only tap the green ones. One wrong tap and you lose a life. Train your brain to filter fast.

Dual Hunt
Two target colors. Multiple decoys. The ultimate test of focus and reflexes. Can you track two colors in the chaos?

FEATURES

- Progressive difficulty — rounds get harder as you advance
- 16 achievements to unlock — from Speed Demon to Perfectionist
- Detailed stats — track your accuracy, reaction time, and streaks
- Sound effects — satisfying audio feedback for every tap
- Dark theme — easy on the eyes, beautiful neon aesthetic
- Privacy-first — no accounts, no tracking, no ads
- Offline play — no internet required
- 3 free rounds per mode — unlock unlimited play for just $1.99

HOW IT WORKS

Dots appear on screen. Tap the right ones before time runs out. Miss 3 and it's game over. Each round increases the challenge with more dots, faster timers, and trickier color combinations.

PERFECT FOR

- Quick gaming sessions on the go
- Training your reflexes and reaction time
- Competing with friends for high scores
- Anyone who loves simple but addictive arcade games

Download DotHunter now and find out how fast you really are.

## Google Play Console Upload Steps

1. Go to https://play.google.com/console
2. Create a new app → "DotHunter"
3. Fill in store listing with text above
4. Upload icon, feature graphic, and screenshots
5. Set pricing: Free with in-app purchases
6. Complete content rating questionnaire (IARC)
7. Add privacy policy URL
8. Upload the signed APK/AAB
9. Submit for review

## Important Notes

- Google requires a **one-time $25 developer registration fee** for Play Console
- First review typically takes 1-7 days
- For in-app purchases, you'll need to set up Google Play Billing in the app
- The current APK uses a debug keystore — for production, generate a proper signing key
